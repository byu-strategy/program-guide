AI Developer Conference

The AI Developer Conference (April 28–29 at Pier 48, SF) features hands-on workshops, coding labs, startup showcases, and keynotes from industry leaders including Andrew Ng. Student passes and volunteer positions are available.

For Students

Join ~31 BYU Product Management students for an immersive trip to Silicon Valley. Visit top tech companies, attend the AI Developer Conference, and network with BYU PM alumni working at leading firms.

Complete the forms below to secure your spot. The $25 program fee covers administrative costs and is due by the application deadline.

For Companies

Thank you for hosting BYU's Product Management students. Our students are pursuing MBAs and Masters in Product Management at the BYU Marriott School of Business and bring strong analytical, technical, and leadership skills.

Each visit typically runs 60–90 minutes and can include an office tour, a product/team overview, and Q&A with your team. We're happy to tailor the format to what works best for you.
